Future of Sense

Welcome to Sense, a Unix-based operating system designed specifically for blind and elderly people. In this section, we explore the directions and visions that guide the continued development of this platform.

Our goal is clear: create an operating system that not only meets the current needs of our users but also evolves to adapt to the challenges and opportunities of the future.

Our Objectives

1. Accessibility without limits
2. Simplicity and ease of use
3. Security and privacy
4. Sustainability and compatibility
